KB Navigator

The KB Navigator allows the specification of decisions by selecting one of their respective outcomes. Impacts of a selected outcome are depicted through the corresponding including and excluding relationships as well as requiring relations between decisions. Occuring conflicts through the user's choices will be highlighted and warnings might be raised. The depiction of requiring relations between decisions and whether the relations of all selected outcomes or only of the last chosen outcome should be shown can be toggled with the respective controls. The same applies to the visualization of additional relationship types that do not directly influence the selections.
