Hierarchical Layout

The hierarchical layot shows the structure of CloudDSF+ with decision points, decisions and outcomes. For easier navigation all mentionded elements can be toggled at once with the respective button.

Decision Relations Layout

The decision relations layout shows all decisions of CloudDSF+ clustered by their respective decision points. All identified relationship types can be toggled through the respective controls. Clicking on a decision will highlight its outgoing relations while fading out everything else.

Outcome Relations Layout

This outcome relations layout shows all outcomes of CloudDSF+ grouped by their decision as well as decision point. Clicking on an outcome will highlight its outgoing relations depending on the selection of relationship types that can be toggled with the respective controls. All outcomes can be selected or deselected at once, for instance, to show all including relations between outcomes. Double clicking a decision or decision point will collapse it to enable a more coarse or fine grained view. In addition, the layout can be fixed or loosened to enable a fast and easy rearranging.
