KB Navigator

This visualization allows a selection of the outcomes of CloudDSF+ and shows the relations between them. Occuring conflicts through the user's choices will be highlighted and warnings might be raised. The visualization of additional relationship types that do not directly influence possible selections can be toggled with the respective controls.
